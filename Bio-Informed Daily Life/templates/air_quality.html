Understanding VOCs and Your Health

Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) from your MQ-135 sensor can impact human DNA subtly. While not immediately alarming, prolonged exposure may affect cellular processes.

Actionable Tips

  • Open windows for fresh air circulation.
  • Use exhaust fans in kitchens and bathrooms.
  • Choose low-VOC products for cleaning and painting.
